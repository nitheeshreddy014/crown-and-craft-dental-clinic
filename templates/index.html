FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dental services and treatments.

How often should I visit the dentist?
+

We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for a routine check-up and professional cleaning. However, if you have specific dental conditions, Dr. Maneesh Reddy Pocharam may suggest more frequent visits tailored to your needs.

Is the root canal treatment painful?
+

Modern root canal treatment is virtually painless. At Crown & Craft Dental Clinic, we use advanced anesthesia techniques and the latest equipment to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure. Most patients report feeling no pain during treatment.

How long do dental implants last?
+

With proper care and regular dental check-ups, dental implants can last a lifetime. The implant crown may need replacement after 10-15 years due to normal wear. Dr. Maneesh Reddy Pocharam uses premium-quality implants for maximum durability.

What age should my child first visit the dentist?
+

The American Dental Association recommends that children visit the dentist by their first birthday or within 6 months of their first tooth erupting. Early visits help establish good oral hygiene habits and allow early detection of potential issues.

How much does teeth whitening cost?
+

Professional teeth whitening at Crown & Craft Dental Clinic starts from ₹5,000. The exact cost depends on the whitening method and the number of sessions required. We offer both in-office and take-home whitening options for your convenience.

Do you offer emergency dental services?
+

Yes! We understand dental emergencies can happen anytime. Crown & Craft Dental Clinic provides emergency dental services for issues like severe toothaches, broken or knocked-out teeth, and dental infections. Contact us immediately for urgent care.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and major health insurance plans. We also offer EMI options for expensive treatments. Our goal is to make quality dental care accessible and affordable for everyone.

How long does the Braces/Aligners treatment take?
+

The duration of orthodontic treatment varies depending on the complexity of your case. Traditional braces typically take 12-24 months, while clear aligners may take 6-18 months. Dr. Maneesh Reddy Pocharam will provide a personalized treatment timeline during your consultation.
